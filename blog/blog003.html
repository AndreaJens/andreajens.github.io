I watched The Amazing Digital Circus

After many people suggested I do that, I finally sat down in the German heat (32+ degrees Celsius at home) to watch The Amazing Digital Circus. 

I went in with basically no expectations, I didn't know what would wait for me since I kept myself almost purposely blind.

Yet, it surprisingly struck a chord. I cried during the final episode, really, as the dumb adult idiot I am.

There's so much I'd love to say about this series, but this is not the place or the time. Just that I could relate with many of the characters.

The insecurity, the panic attacks, the subtle imagery for depression...

It's probably weird to say, but I don't think I'll forget this series soon.
