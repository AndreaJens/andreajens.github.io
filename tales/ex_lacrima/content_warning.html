Ex Lacrima Remnant

⚠️Content warning⚠️

This novel contains themes and situations that might be considered inappropriate or distressful for some readers. Such content includes:

  • (Attempted) suicide

  • Swear words/crude language

  • Suggestive themes

  • Nudity

  • Sexual innuendo, consensual sex (not explicit), after-sex scenes

  • Drug (ab)use, withdrawal

  • Violence

  • Blood, injuries, severed limbs, decapitation, death

  • Body horror

  • Fantasy racism

  • Child and animal deaths (implied, not described explicitly)

To avoid story spoilers, the content of each chapter is left unmarked.
