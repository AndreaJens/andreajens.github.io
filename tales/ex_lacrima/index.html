Ex Lacrima Remnant (2024)

"Ex Lacrima Remnant" is a gargantuan soft sci-fi novel featuring ax-crazy violinists, metalheaded himbos, flying mechs, weird scientists and lesbian plant girls - a whole lot of them - on a distant planet where humanity began a new life. It's an excessive, gorey, horny novel bordering on NSFW but never falling into it heads-deep and, by far, my most divisive and weirdest opus - one I'm honestly both proud and scared of. This page contains the table of content. Every chapter is linked to the next through the bottom bar, so that you can enjoy it at your own pace without having to skim through an endless behemoth of a single web page. I hope you'll enjoy the ride as much as I enjoyed writing it - and that you won't hate me for this. I will upload the chapters weekly starting from March 22, 2025, until the very last Chapter 76. The first batch of 5 chapters and 1 interlude is available already! Make sure to check this page regularly for updates!
