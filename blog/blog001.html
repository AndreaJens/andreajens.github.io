What is Fox Fire Rondeau, really?

Some of you might have noticed me mentioning a work of fiction called "Fox Fire Rondeau" that cannot be found on this very website.

Understandable – after all, it's clearly brought up in Exploding Judo Federation as Apple's origin story – a chaotic in-universe NSFW action novel that is nothing more than a tongue-in-cheek joke.

Well, the problem is that Fox Fire Rondeau actually exists and is as long as Ex Lacrima Remnant as of today, courtesy of my long time behind-the-curtains collaborator Lyon Vida.

Should you read it? Well, of course it's up to you, but if you fancy a crazy action story with gunplay, hot foxes, murder robots and lots of exposed skin, you're welcome to head for either itch.io, Archive of Our Own, or even (gulp!) Wattpad. The covers have been drawn by another long time collaborator of mine, Eliz Bee.

It's free, it exists, and it's as deranged as you might imagine.

I won't post it on this website because it is strictly mature content and not suitable for this venue. You are welcome to archive it and share it from the links above, though.

If you do, leave good ol' Lyon a comment – he'll surely be happy about it.
