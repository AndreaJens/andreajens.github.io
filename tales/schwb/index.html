Tales from Schwarzerblitz (2020-2024)

Between 2016 and 2024 I developed a 3D fighting game called Schwarzerblitz, which featured a 5-hours long story mode shown via 3D renders and visual novel styled dialogue. During this time, I started to write short stories set during and after the events of the game. The first batch is now finished and spans more than 40 entries, for a grand total of more than 330k words. I have recently uploaded most of them to AO3 for safekeeping, but I figured I could also publish them on my own website to avoid them going AWOL and have more redundancy, if possible. Funnily enough, these stories are directly referencing Distortionverse events and - at some point - are hijacked by the very same Veckert Rainer that accompanied me for so long.
