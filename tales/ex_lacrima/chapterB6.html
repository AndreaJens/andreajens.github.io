#B-Side VI – Welcome to Neon

You are just a gear in the system.

But you can change it.

Come to Neon!

No matter where you are from, be it Eastern Coalition, New Netherlands, the Pangean Union or the Shuraya Archipelago – as long as you have a can-do attitude, Neon will welcome you with open arms. Religion, skin color, language – none of them matter on the Moon – only what you bring.

Developed on an area of three thousand square kilometers and hosting more than ten million people, Neon is the new frontier of humanity, where dreams become reality and the past is a relic long forgotten.

The reduced gravity makes your life easier, your movements faster. You can’t bear it? No problems, thanks to the practical ring habitats, keeping your constant, familiar acceleration, where you can live till you grow accustomed to your new home. You won’t ever feel like you’ve left Lagash, except that you have.

And you’re now in a better place.

Rockets for Neon depart from more than two hundred authorized spaceports around the world, in neutral areas under the sole jurisdiction of the United Nations of Lagash. Everyone can apply for relocation, even you. Former convicts, people looking for a new life, adventure-seekers…

There’s room for each and every one of you among us.

Book your trip today, contact your local consulate for further information, plan your new life.

And become one of us.

Neon.

Where everybody has a chance to become somebody.
